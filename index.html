Biography

I am a Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department (DCC) at Universidade Federal de Minas Gerais (UFMG), Brazil. I am also a lecturer in the Computer and Systems Department (DECSI) at Universidade Federal de Ouro Preto. Before that I received my M.Sc. from UFMG. And before that I received my B.Sc. in Computer Science from Universidade Federal de Uberlândia (UFU), Brazil.
